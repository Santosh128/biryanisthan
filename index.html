Featured Dishes

Chicken Biryani

 120

Biriyani originated in ancient persia . But when it came to Indian peninsula in medieval period, it went through a complete transformation. The availability of huge range of aromatic spices magically transformed the dish into a truly heavenly dish ,which was only fit for kings in those period. Nowadays biriyani is arguably the most popular dish in india and Hydrabadi dum Biriyani being the best among it all.
We specialize in making of Hydrabadi Dum Biriyani, with our own unique secret masala recipie which when fused with Dum cooking process , produces the best kind of Dum Biriyani you could ever taste

MUTTON CURRY

 220

Odiya style mutton curry or mutton jholo as its popularly known, is one of the tastiest mutton curry prepared in the country.

Made out of pure khassi goat meat which is supposedly the softest meat found in india, mixed with our own masalas and cooked in low heat to produce this tasty dish. We specialize in this wonderful dish from odisha, which goes very well with plain rice.

Chicken Kassa

 165

We also specialize in mutton kassa and chicken kassa which is khassi mutton or chicken cooked with ginger garlic and onion paste in low heat with very mild garam masalas.

It has a dry or thick gravy which goes very well with our soft and fluffy Paratha.

GALAOUTI KEBAB

 165

Galouti Kebabs originated in Lucknow for the nawabs of the medieval period. The kebabs are so soft that it melts in the mouth once it is eaten and was appreciated a lot by the High command of East India Company in the old times. We specialize in making of this delicious soft Galouti kebabs which could go very well with our soft and fluffy Paratha.
